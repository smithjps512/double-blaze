## How it works (plain steps)

1. Tell us about your business. Our intake takes a few minutes and our AI does most of the work.
2. We build it. A real website, your store, your daily posts, plus one workflow we automate for you, AI customer support, inventory, and a live results dashboard.
3. You run it for 30 days, starting the day it goes live, not the day you sign up.
4. We check in along the way and show you exactly what it is producing: leads, sales, hours saved.
5. On day 31 you decide. Continue, move up or down a level, or stop. You are only charged if you stay.

We do ask for a card at signup so that nothing breaks if you continue. You will not be charged until day 31, and you can stop anytime before then in one click.

## Why we do this

We would rather show you than tell you. Plenty of companies will take a year of your money before you have seen a single result. We do the work first. If it does not earn its place in your business in 30 days, you owe us nothing. That confidence is the whole point.

The onboarding you go through is also the best demo we have. The same automation that gets you set up so smoothly is the kind of automation we build into your business.

## Objection handlers

- "Why do you need my card?" So month two is seamless if you continue. No charge until day 31, cancel anytime before then.
- "What if I am a big franchise?" Does not matter. If you run your location, the offer is yours.
- "What if it is not working for me?" Then you stop, and you owe nothing. We check in so you always know where you stand.
- "What if I stop, then change my mind?" We hold your build for 90 days. Log back in with your email, verify it is you, make your first payment, and you are live again right where you left off.
- "Why only 30 days free?" Because the clock starts when your solution goes live, so you get a full month of real results, not a month of waiting on a build.
